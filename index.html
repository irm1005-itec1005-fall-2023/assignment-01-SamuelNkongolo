Our Leader

Pain is the leader of the Akatsuki aswell as one of the Main Villans. He was born in the Hidden Rain Village, where he resides for majority of the show. He was taken in and looked after by one of the three legendary Sanin, Jiraiya. He posses the rinnegan (A powerful eye weapon) and he seeks out peace in his own unique way.
